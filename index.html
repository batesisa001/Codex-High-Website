About The Firm

Thoughtful real estate investing backed by rigor and partnership.

Boardwalk Capital is a private equity real estate firm based in Alpine, Utah. We are focused on identifying compelling opportunities where disciplined analysis, well-structured execution, and active ownership can support long-term value creation.

Our approach is rooted in careful underwriting, local market knowledge, and a selective acquisition strategy. We seek opportunities where alignment matters, downside protection is considered early, and thoughtful asset plans can create a stronger operating and investment outcome over time.

We aim to be a reliable partner to investors, property owners, brokers, and business counterparties by bringing preparation, responsiveness, and a steady long-term perspective to every relationship.

Investment Strategy

A disciplined framework for selecting and operating real estate investments.

Boardwalk Capital emphasizes clarity at each stage of the investment process, from sourcing and underwriting through execution and ongoing asset management.

Targeted Acquisitions

We pursue opportunities that fit a defined investment thesis, with selectivity around basis, location, demand drivers, and operational potential.

Value-Add Opportunities

We look for assets where thoughtful capital planning, leasing strategy, or operational improvement may unlock stronger performance.

Market-Driven Strategy

Market context matters. We evaluate submarket trends, competitive positioning, and downside resilience before capital is committed.

Active Asset Management

We believe value is created through consistent follow-through, clear business plans, and practical oversight after acquisition.
